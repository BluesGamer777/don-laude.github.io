About the Artist

Beauty in service
of the Gospel

Don Laude is a studio devoted to sacred and devotional art — work made not only to be admired, but to draw the heart toward God.

From digital illustrations and fine art prints to children's books and custom commissions, every piece begins in prayer and is shaped by the conviction that beauty is a path to the divine.

— Soli Deo Gloria
